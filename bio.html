Bio

Kathryn is a junior at Tufts University, studying computer science and child development. She is originally from West Virginia, and went to George Washington High School in Charleston. She has worked for Girls Who Code and the West Virginia Purchasing Division.

On the Tufts campus, you can find Kathryn volunteering with Girl Scouts, leading a small group with the Protestant Student Association, and working with incoming students as a peer leader with the CAFE pre-orientation program. She enjoys reading, fiber arts, and creating spotify playlists.
